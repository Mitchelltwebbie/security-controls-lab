\# Control Card Template



\*\*Control ID:\*\* CC-XXX  

\*\*Control Name:\*\*  

\*\*Framework Mapping (optional):\*\* PCI / SOC 2 / ISO (high level)



\## 1) Objective (the “why”)

What risk does this reduce? What outcome are we trying to guarantee?



\## 2) Scope (what it covers)

What systems/assets does this apply to?

What is explicitly out of scope?



\## 3) Frequency (how often)

Daily / Weekly / Monthly / Quarterly / On-change



\## 4) Owner (who runs it)

Who performs the control? Who reviews it? (in your lab, it’s you)



\## 5) Procedure (the “how”)

List the steps clearly enough that a stranger could follow them.



\## 6) Evidence (the “proof”)

What exact artifacts prove the control happened?

Where are they saved in the repo?



\## 7) Exceptions / Notes

When is the control allowed to be skipped or delayed?

What compensating control exists?
